Scoring Metrics
Total Score — Net economic benefit: Total Savings minus Total Fees. This is the primary ranking metric (higher is better).
Total Savings — Sum of savings generated from correctly predicted core items (10% of historical spend on matched items).
Total Fees — Fixed fee of €10 charged per predicted item per month, regardless of whether the prediction was correct.
Num Hits — Number of your predicted (buyer, product) pairs that match actual core demand items in the ground truth.
Spend Capture Rate — Fraction of the total ground-truth core-demand spend that your predictions successfully captured.
Scoring Parameters
Savings Rate: 10%
Fixed Fee: €10.00
Scoring Months: 1
These parameters may be adjusted by the organizers during the hackathon.
